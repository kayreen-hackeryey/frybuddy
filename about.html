How FryPals Works

FryPals works by converting used cooking oil into liquid soap using a semi-automatic process. It’s simple and safe, allowing you to recycle waste oil into a useful product in just a few minutes.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We are committed to reducing environmental impact by providing a practical solution to used cooking oil disposal. FryPals turns waste into a resource, helping reduce pollution and waste.

Why Choose FryPals?

  • Easy to use – No complicated processes or machinery required.
  • Eco-friendly – Reduce waste, help the environment.
  • Cost-effective – Make soap at home with minimal costs.
